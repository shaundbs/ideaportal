Portal Managers

Responsible for user management and org customisation


  • Can create, edit and delete users less senior in the permission hierarchy (Public/NHS staff users)
  • Has the ability to customise orgs with information of their choosing
  • Controls the posts that are displayed to users
  • Full-access to other organisation's portals

Other User Types

  • Admins
  • Challenge managers
  • NHS Staff
  • Public
